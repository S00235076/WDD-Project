About Us

RetroUnited.ie was established in late 2023 by an Irish Manchester United fan who loved the old retro jerseys but found it hard to find somewhere to purchase them.

So he decided to create a platform to make buying retro jerseys easier in Ireland and with cheaper delivery

The company is located in Sligo but delivers to all over the Republic of Ireland
